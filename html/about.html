Hi

I’m Rebecca Malagisi, a graphic and web designer based in Montreal, Canada. I am currently majoring in the BFA Design program at Concordia University. My studies have led me particularly towards an interest in web design, as I enjoy exploring how a webpage can be used as a creative and interactive space. I also enjoy multimedia approaches to design, such as creating and editing video, photography, and audio. I always strive to improve my design process through practice and collaboration.

When I am not working on my designs, I am often inspired by painting, sculpture, and textile arts (embroidery, weaving, and more). These forms of tactile creation help to expand my interests beyond simply a screen based form of art.
