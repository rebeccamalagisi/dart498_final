Blue Print

Interactive Installation Concept

November - December 2016

Montreal residents often complain that construction projects around the city are constantly ongoing or do not get completed on time, interfering with their daily lives. The project would invite the public to help determine which construction projects they would like to be completed in a timely manner. The aim is to help the public feel included and feel like their voices have an impact, helping relieve some of the frustration towards the construction workers. The project would display two projects of similar nature on a large touch screen; for example, two projects that are repairing pipelines. Users can approach the screen and vote by touching the vote button of one’s preferred choice and the results would be displayed on the screen in real time. Once a vote has been cast, a new set of projects will be displayed on the screen.

Technical - Blender 3D, Unity 3D, Adobe InDesign, Adobe After Effects

Rebecca Malagisi - graphic design, video editing
