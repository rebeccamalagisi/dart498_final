Failures: A didascalogue

Book Design

March-April 2015

Book design for the play "Failures: A didascalogue" by Louis Patrick Leroux. As a team, we wanted our design to reflect the story's underlying theme of growing tension and disorder. Using a design grid throughout each page, we were able to visually build tension amongst the first spreads by making the chapter columns continuously narrower, and then later breaking the tension and introducing more chaos by changing the format of each page based on the contents of the chapter.

Technical: Adobe InDesign, Adobe Illustrator, Adobe Photoshop

Rebecca Malagisi: book design, book binding

Jade Morgan: illustrations, book design

All: concept development
