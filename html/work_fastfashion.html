Fast Fashion

Interactive Website

March - April 2016

This project explores the negative aspects of the fashion industry through an interactive website. Clothes are a basic necessity for humans; like food, water, and shelter, garments contribute to the health and well-being of the person wearing them. Clothing has the ability to give us confidence, and allows us to choose how we want others to see ourselves and represent who we are. However, the rate of consumption and purging of no longer wanted items is creating many concerns, including environmental aspects and human right issues. Solutions are offered to no longer contribute to the fast fashion industry.

Technical - HTML5, CSS3, jQuery
