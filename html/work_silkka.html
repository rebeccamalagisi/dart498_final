Silkka Handmade Soap

Product Design - Fundraising Project

February - April 2016

This project had come to life with the following questions in mind: How do we create a product that will attract potential buyers in order to raise money for local non-profit organizations? How can we create a sustainable product that produces as little waste as possible? Through brainstorming, our team had decided to create our own soap, as it is a commonly used item that offers a sense of renewal. Silkka - inspired by the Finnish word for simple, sheer, and pure - intends to encompass all these qualities in its product and brand. Our handmade cold process soaps are made with natural and simple ingredients. 100% cotton knitted pouch house each soap to minimize unsustainable packaging, as well as act as a gentle exfoliator to use with the soap. Produced for the design pop-up shop fundraiser at Concordia University, all proceeds of 40+ soaps sold were donated to Chez Doris women’s shelter, promoting a safe space for women.
