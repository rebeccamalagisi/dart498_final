Silkka Handmade Soap

Product Design - Fundraising Project

February - April 2016

This project had come to life with the following questions in mind: How do we create a product that is commonly used and will attract the attention of potential buyers, all while embedding the stylistic approach to northern design? How can it be sustainable and create as little waste as possible? Silkka - inspired by the Finnish word for simple, sheer, and pure - intends to encompass all these qualities in its product and brand. Our handmade cold process soaps are made with natural and simple ingredients. 100% cotton knitted pouch house each soap to minimize unsustainable packaging, as well as act as a gentle exfoliator to use with the soap.
